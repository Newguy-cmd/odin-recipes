Ingredients

  • 12 ounces fettuccine or other long pasta
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/4 up( 1/2 stick) unsalted butter
  • 3/4 upfinely grated Parmesan ; plus more for serving
  • Freshly ground black pepper
>

Directions

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until very al dente. Drain, reserving 2 cups pasta cooking liquid.
  2. Transfer 1 cup pasta cooking liquid to a large skillet. Bring to a gentle simmer, then whisk in butter, a piece at a time, until melted. Whisking constantly, gradually add cheese, making sure itâ€™s completely melted and incorporated before adding more.
  3. Add pasta and toss to coat, adding more pasta cooking liquid as needed, until sauce blankets noodles completely.
  4. Serve topped with pepper and more cheese.
